# Privacy & Safety Copy

## In-app copy (recommended)
- "This app is for tracking only. It does not provide medical advice."
- "Serum level graphs are visual estimates and may be inaccurate."

## Data handling
- Do not transmit user data off-device in v1
- If iCloud sync added, disclose it clearly

## Optional protections
- FaceID lock (future)
- Local encryption (future; iOS device encryption covers most use cases)
